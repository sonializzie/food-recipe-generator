  1. Crack the egg into a bowl and whisk it lightly.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the diced tomato to the pan and cook for about 2-3 minutes, until they start to soften.
  4. Pour the whisked egg over the tomatoes in the pan.
  5. Let the egg cook for about 2-3 minutes, until the edges start to set.
  6. Use a spatula to gently lift and fold the edges of the egg towards the center, allowing the uncooked egg to flow to the edges.
  7. Continue cooking for another 1-2 minutes, until the egg is almost set.
  8. Slide the frittata out of the pan onto a plate and serve hot.
